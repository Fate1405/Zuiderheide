Rabbit

Oryctolagus cuniculus

JJ Harrison/ CC-BY-SA-3.0

The European rabbit is a small mammal belonging to the rabbit family. Found throughout Europe, these furry creatures are wont to digging intricate holes and tunnel systems known as burrows or warrens. They are typically gregarious and have strict hierarchies amongst males and females.

They live around grasslands and their herbivorous diet consists mainly of grasses and berries. They have many natural predators including dogs, cats, Mustelidae and raptors.
